THE RED VELVET CAKE RECIPE WAS AN ACT OF REVENGE

One of the interesting cake facts about Red Velvet is that the recipe was made known to the public as an act of revenge. A woman ate the Red Velvet cake in the restaurant and got quite fond of it.
